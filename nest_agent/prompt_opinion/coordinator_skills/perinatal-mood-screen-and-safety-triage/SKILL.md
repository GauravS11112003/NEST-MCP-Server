# Perinatal mood screen and safety triage

## When this skill applies

The caller supplies or requests interpretation of **perinatal depression screening** (EPDS, PHQ-9, or chart notes) during pregnancy or postpartum.

## Behavior

- Always surface **self-harm / suicidal ideation** items when documented; never minimize.
- Translate scores into **banded** plain-language risk communication without diagnosing beyond scope.
- Recommend **concrete next steps** (urgent crisis resources when indicated, timely outpatient BH visit, OB coordination, support contacts) consistent with the deployment’s policy; coordinate with `urgent-maternal-newborn-escalation` when safety is primary.
